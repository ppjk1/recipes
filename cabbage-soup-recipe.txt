From http://comfortablefood.com/super-easy-beef-cabbage-soup/

INGREDIENTS
===========
  butter - 1 tbsp
  ground beef - 1 pound, lean
  garlic salt - 1/2 tsp
  garlic powder - 1/2 tsp
  pepper - 1/4 tsp
  celery stalks - 2, chopped fine
  kidney beans - 1 16 oz can, not drained
  cabbage - 1/2 head (medium), chopped
  tomatoes - 1 can (28 oz), including liquid
  water - 1 tomato can worth
  beef bouillon - 4 cubes
  parsley - fresh, for garnish

DIRECTIONS
==========
  1. Brown the beef in the butter in a Dutch oven over medium heat.

  2. Add all the remaining ingredients except the parsley; bring to a boil.

  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour.

  4. Serve garnished with fresh parsley.
